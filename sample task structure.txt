--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Heading Paragraph
  - Describe the issue
  - Pivot to prompt
  - Thesis sentence + essay structure sentence
Body Paragraphs
  - Topic sentence
  - Example or hypotnese
  - correlation of thesis with example
Conclusion Paragraph 
 (for strongly agree)
  - give a counter point and then shut them up
  - give conclusion of thesis sentence
 (for mostly agree)
  - say that the thesis is true is most of cases but some cases/people have different opinion on it
  - give conclusion of thesis sentence
